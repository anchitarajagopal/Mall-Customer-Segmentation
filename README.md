In this project, we figure out who the best customers are. 
  Explore the general distribution of the data to get a sense of Male vs. Female customers, and how their income, age, and spending scores are similar or different.
  Explore which gender has a higher income.
  Explore which gender has a higher average spending score.
  
Setup:
You will need to have numpy, pandas, seaborn, and matplotlib.

The data set is from Kaggle and can be downloaded from this repo.
